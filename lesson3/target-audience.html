Description

Audience segments such as the Business Traveler, the Family Vacationers, the Friends' Getaway, and the International Jetsetter, plan their getaways differently, and travel brands have opportunity to connect with them through effective targeted marketing at different stages of their planning cycle.

  • General information about the city, typical places, history, why visit it, what it offers to tourism.
  • Weather information, recommended routes, special offers according to the different seasons, high and low.

Adult Marriage

Adult couples, economically well established, who take vacations once or twice a year, who are looking to discover stories, landscapes, foods and regional places.

Young Adults

Young adults, economically not well established, who are looking for affordable prices and who take vacations once a year with other friends, who are looking to discover landscapes, food, places and nightlife.
